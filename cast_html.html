Cast of the Film

JASON STATHAM as PIERRE BEZUKHOV An introverted, sensitive young man who unexpectedly inherits a huge estate. It changes his life - and turns him into a mark.
EMILIA CLARKE as NATASHA ROSTOVA The beautiful youngest daughter of the Rostov family.
GERARD BUTLER AS ANDREY BOLKONSKY An admirer of Napoleon, the cerebral Prince Andrey is a success at everything he tries...except for love.
PETER DINKLAGE as NAPOLEON BONAPARTE The greatest conqueror in history, determined to spread liberty, equality, and fraternity across Europe...just don't mention his height.
ED SHEERAN as NIKOLAI ROSTOV A headstrong young officer from the Russian gentry, with a good heart but a lot left to learn.
EMMA WATSON as HELENE KURAGINA A femme fatale out for Pierre's heart, and his money.
SKEPTA as ANATOL KURAGIN Pierre's brother-in-law, the bad boy of St. Petersburg. Some say he's good for nothing, but he's a loving brother - VERY loving.
